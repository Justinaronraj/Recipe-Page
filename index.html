Lasagne alla Bolognese

Alla Bolognese is a popular dish all over the world, featuring multiple layers of beef, tomato, and onion bolognese sauce, bechamel sauce, and lasagne pasta sheets until the cheesy top. This is a rich and warming dish that goes perfectly with a bold red Italian wine. This dish was invented in the city of Bologna in the Emilia region, and it has risen to immense popularity. It has become somewhat of an Italian institution, and many people consider it to represent the nation.
